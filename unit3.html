Part 2: Reading

15 Points

Dialogue

Shop assistant: Good morning. Can I help you?

Woman: Yes, please. I'm on holiday and I'm looking for a bag.

Assistant: Oh, lovely. Are you from London?

Woman: Yes, I am.

Assistant: It's a nice city.

Woman: Thank you. That bag is beautiful. Where is it from?

Assistant: We have things from China, Mexico, Spain, and more. That one is from Italy. It's £45.

Woman: And those sunglasses?

Assistant: They're from Germany. These ones are £22.

Woman: I'll take the bag and the sunglasses. That's a good price — £60 for both?

Assistant: Yes, that's £60 for both. Do you want anything else?

Woman: No, thanks. I'll pay by bank card.

Assistant: Bank cards are fine. Thank you!
